Zandism Contemporary Art

Zand is a contemporary artist that is based in the UK. He wanted a website in order to display his work and at a later stage sell his creations.

Website was marked-up with HTML5 semantic elements, styled with CSS3, functionality with a combination of Javascript and Jquery and then turned into a Wordpress theme in order to make use of the blog and store functionality.

Technology Used:

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • Javascript
  • Wordpress
